What is it?

lunadrop is a comprehensive drone delivery system. Immediately after you place an order on our website, we pack everything up for you and strap the items to one of our luna drones. The drone flies autonomously from our central hub to the your requested landing site, safely descends to drop off your package, and flies back to our central hub where it awaits the next order.

How does it work?

The lunadrop project can best be broken down into three main parts.

The Website

lunadrop.com and the web server sitting behind it are the portal to rapid autonomous drone delivery. We have built an intuitive and mobile-friendly web interface that allows you to easily place an order and watch your drone's progress as it flies to you in real-time.

The Drones

lunadrop autonomous delivery vehicles are custom unmanned aerial systems. Each drone is outfitted with our custom package gripper designed and built at Brown University. An open source, ARM-based flight controller in union with an onboard Linux computer allow the drone to communicate with our server over WiFi or LTE and navigate autonomously.

The Manager

The Manager is the brain of the system. It recieves your order from the server and intelligently assigns it to an available drone, all while taking into account factors such as distance, remaining battery life, and payload weight. After generating a flight plan and dispatching a drone, it actively monitors its in-flight performance, location, and status throughout the duration of its flight.
